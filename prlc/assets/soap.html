Pine Ridge Soap

Began June 30, 2016

We began making hot process lye soap to help with skin problems my daughter and I had. Also, I found it fun. Well, after a month, my skin didn't hurt and my daughter, Kyla, no longer had white flaky, itchy, dry skin. Also I had a 40 yr old mole fell off! The Tea Tree oil claims to have anti-fungal properties and I also noted after 2 months my feet no longer itched or had skin peeling off between my toes! I thought by using NATURAL ingredients instead of harmful ones like “Triclosan” the FDA just banned, that this would Help folks! I used to watch my Grandma make lye soap in the basement. We make soap and “bag & tag” each one according to “kind & intended purpose” along with our business card. Lye is mixed into water and is immediately turned into SOAP when it mixes with our 3 triglycerides: Lard, Coconut oil, & Soybean oil. (except in Castile soap (virgin olive oil soap). The Lye is GONE! First, this “mixture” goes to “trace”. Then, after heating, begins the “saponification” process:
1) Goes hard,
2) Goes waxy – which is the beginning of making into soap. Next, after cooking, a temperature drop to certain degrees for adding fragrances & oils (or not, for scentless “lye” soap).
A Major Fact about Lye Soap is that it Isn't Lye Soap!!! The “lye water” goes thru a chemical reaction where it isn't lye anymore!!! Kinda like H2 meeting up with O then becoming H2O: a gas meeting another gas = water ! So, lye + triglycerides = SOAP !!! No lye ! (Ha, ha, no pun intended)
We make & sell almost any kind of soap 1+1/16” thick x 3.75”L x 2.5 to 2.75”W , 6 oz. bar with any kind of essential oils for $3.75. (bulk sale: negotiable) Examples:

1) Eucalyptus, Lemon Grass, Tea Tree, --- with --- Shea Nut oil, Glycerin, Vit E (with mix of one or more of: Turmeric, Kelp, Spirulina, Beet root, Madder root –- or not)
2) Virgin Olive Oil, Lavender, Peppermint, Glycerin
3) Cinnamon, Cassia, Clove, Shea Nut Oil, Glycerin, Vit. E
4) Basic soap – no added fragrances-- a plain lye soap (or a tiny amt. of lavender & tea tree). This in a 13/16” thick, 3.75”L x 2.50”W, 4 oz. bar sells for Only $1.50 (bulk sale, $1.25)
